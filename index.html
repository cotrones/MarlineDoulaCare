The Company

Committed to helping women meet their goals through this exciting journey. Maintaining a small client load to personalize continuous care together by creating a team to help you welcome this little one into the world.

Services

Services

A doula is a non-medical person who is trained to care for families throughout pregnancy, birth and postpartum. Providing emotional, physical and educational support and be present to support the entire family to achieve the experience they dream of. Having someone who understands the changes your body is experiencing, and able to suggest ways to make you more comfortable especially with a long and challenging labor. A doula will not speak for you, but will help create a comfortable environment for families to speak for themselves.

Basic Package

$700

Consultation

During this visit we will gather information regarding your birth preferences so it can be tailored to you needs.

Services Include
  • A prenatal visit to introduce your medical staff to your doula prior to labor
  • 24/7 access to your doula via text, email and/or telephone
  • Your doula will be on call starting weeks 38 through 42
  • When labor begins and intensifies your doula will support your family through labor, birth and postpartum
  • A home visit when your family is settled in so we can revisit any questions you my have and to admire your little one
